FOOD TRUCK

Role: Software Engineer Involved in Frontend and Backend Implementation

FoodTruck is an online food ordering and delivery web application designed to simplify the process of ordering and paying for food from a variety of restaurants. Users can conveniently browse menus, place orders, and make secure payments through integrated payment platforms like Paystack or Flutterwave. Additionally, restaurants can create accounts, manage their menus and prices, confirm orders, and facilitate deliveries, all while providing real-time updates to users. FoodTruck aims to revolutionize the food delivery industry by providing a seamless and efficient platform for both users and restaurants.

Project goal: The problem that FoodTruck aims to address is the inconvenience and inefficiency associated with the traditional process of ordering and paying for food from restaurants.

Solution: FoodTruck addresses these challenges by providing a comprehensive solution that enables users to order and pay for food from various restaurants online, offers restaurants an opportunity to expand their online presence, and facilitates order management and delivery coordination. By doing so, FoodTruck aims to make the entire process more convenient, transparent, and efficient for all parties involved
